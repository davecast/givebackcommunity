Our Story

While studying at university in the Spring of 2020, Umberto Rosi and peers decided to hand out food in the streets of Downtown West Palm Beach one Friday evening. It was on this night that Umberto recognized the powerful impact of such a simple gesture. His outlook on life has been changed ever sin ce. Umberto had a vision of starting a movement. A Movement that would not only change the lives of those who were seeking for help, but also of those that would serve as volunteers with one common mission. A Movement dedicated to bringing compassion, hope, and encouragement to those in need. A Movement that teaches our volunteers how to Give Back to our communities through simple and consistent Acts of Generosity.The Give Back Community is an initiative designed to Spread the Power of Generosity. Since 2020, The Give Back Community has collaborated with numerous churches, small businesses, large corporations and community members to join another in service.

This is just the beginning. Live Generously
