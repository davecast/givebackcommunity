Give Back School

Mission

To provide daily education and mentorship to children in need.

Give Back Kids program, The Give Back Community is fully sponsoring a school of over 50 children in Rio de Janeiro. Through our giving, we are able to provide full-time educators and learning materials to children growing up in one of the most dangerous areas in Brazil. Our goal is to impact the lives of children at a young age so that they may become leaders of Generosity worldwide.

game day

Mission

To offer an opportunity for volunteers to Spread Generosity and witness the power of transforming lives.

Game Day is our weekly outreach program that connects volunteers with the opportunity to serve those in need through providing warm meals and conversation. Through our many partnerships, we are able to provide over 3,000 meals every month! By visiting our homeless friends on a consistent schedule, our teams can better understand how we can serve and encourage our friends in need.

giveback jobs

Mission

To connect those willing and ready to work with local businesses and providing employment opportunities and steady income.

Since being founded in 2020, The Give Back Community has provided 23 employment opportunities through Give Back Jobs. We have partnered with many local businesses that offer employment interviews to candidates whom we recommend. After they receive jobs and income, we remain in consistent communication with the individual and the employer to hear about their progress. Give Back jobs is one of the most important aspects of ensuring growth and independence for our friends on the streets.

giveback kids

Mission

To provide recreational activities, mentorship, for disabled children, and kids from challenging backgrounds.

Through our partnership with VIP Kids and Urban Youth Impact, our volunteers have been able to expand their Generosity to reaching children with disabilities and underprivileged kids. Through recreational activities such as tennis, dancing, soccer etc, our team helps raise the spirits, skills, and confidence of children growing up in troubling situations. We simply aim to bring a smiling face and a fun opportunity for children to connect with strong role models.
